    Overview

    Andhra Pradesh, a captivating land where the rhythmic waves of the Bay of Bengal kiss the golden shores, ancient temples echo with devotion, and a rich tapestry of culture and history unfolds amidst lush landscapes and vibrant cities.

    Best time to visit

    Winter

    The weather is pleasant, with cool days and cooler nights. The temperature range is 13° C to 28° C.

    How to Reach Andhra Pradesh

    By Air

    Hyderabad’s Rajiv Gandhi International Airport is the biggest airport of Andhra.

    By Train

    The Hyderabad Deccan Railway Station, also known as Nampally Station, is the busiest railway station of Hyderabad. It is a part of South-Central Railways.

    By Bus

    The Andhra Pradesh State Road Transport Corporation is the state-owned bus service which covers the entire state with regular, luxury, semi-luxury and air-conditioned buses.

    What is Andhra famous for?
    Andhra is famous for having both hills and beaches and temples and outdoor attractions.

    It is indeed one of the safest places in India for traveling.
