    Overview

    Shimla, nestled in the Himalayan foothills, is a captivating hill station where colonial elegance blends with stunning natural beauty, offering a serene escape.

    Best time to visit

    Spring

    The best time to visit Shimla is between March to June when the weather remains pleasant and the temperature ranges from 15°C to 30°C.

    Summer

    The summer months are ideal for visiting famous places and enjoying the city's scenic views and cool winds.

    Winter

    Shimla witnesses heavy snowfall from December to February. October is an excellent month to explore the city with fewer crowds.

    How to Reach Shimla

    By Air

    Shimla Airport or Jubbarhatti Airport, about 20 km from the city, is the closest domestic airport serving this beautiful hill station.

    By Train

    Kalka railway station, situated at a distance of around 96 km, is the nearest railhead connecting Shimla to neighbouring Indian cities.

    By Bus

    A bus journey to Shimla is a visual delight. The hill station enjoys excellent connectivity with neighbouring cities.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    When is the best time to visit Shimla?
    Summer is the best time to visit Shimla for a cool hill station vacation. The weather stays pleasant between the summer months of March and June and all attractions are open and accessible. However, if you want to enjoy snow and experience the thrill of skiing and ice-skating, then winter is the time to be in Shimla.
    Which is the closest airport to Shimla?
    Shimla’s own Jubbarhatti Airport is only 20 km away from the city. But it is not on the regular routes of commercial airlines. Chandigarh Airport is the closest airport to Shimla which is well-connected with the rest of the country. It is around 130km from Shimla and it takes around 3hrs and 45mins by road.
    When does it start snowing in Shimla?
    It starts snowing in Shimla from the end of December onwards.
    How far is Chandigarh from Shimla?
    Chandigarh is around 130km from Shimla and it takes around 3hrs and 45mins to cover the distance by road.
    Is Shimla an expensive destination?
    For what it offers, Shimla is a reasonably-priced holiday destination. The prices may go up during the peak season. But if you book your holidays in advance, you can save money.
