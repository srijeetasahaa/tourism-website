    Overview

    Ladakh is a breathtaking high-altitude region renowned for its dramatic mountain landscapes, serene monasteries, vibrant culture, and unique Tibetan heritage.

    Best time to visit

    Spring

    Spring in Ladakh unveils vibrant landscapes, mild weather, and accessible roads, offering a serene and intimate experience of this breathtaking region.

    How to Reach Ladakh

    By Air

    Fly from Delhi to Leh and enjoy views of the Himalayan mountains in your flight.

    By Train

    The nearest railway station to Ladakh is Jammu Tawi, which is about 700 km away. From Jammu, you can hire a private vehicle to Ladakh.

    By Bus

    You can take a cab, jeep, or bus from Srinagar or Manali. You can also bike to Leh from these cities.

    The best time to visit Ladakh is in the summer, from the end of March to the middle of August. In fact, June is one of the greatest months to visit Ladakh.

    Ladakh is about 3000 meters or 9800 feet above mean sea level.
